Strategic Initiatives and Operational Highlights

  • AI and Technology:
    • AI used for personalized experiences, recommendation models, and operational efficiency
    • AI-driven attach rates at record highs
    • AI speeds up property onboarding (now 70% faster)
    • AI deployed internally for product development, inventory onboarding, and customer service
    • Focus on Expedia brands' prominence in generative AI searches and agentic browsers
  • Product and Brand Initiatives:
    • Launch of "Cancel for Any Reason" assurance product
    • Expansion of VrboCare for traveler peace of mind
    • New Brand Expedia campaign: "The One Place You Go to Go Places" (Super Bowl ad, 200M+ YouTube views)
    • Brand positioning: Expedia as one-stop shop, Hotels.com as hotel pure play, Vrbo as trusted vacation rental marketplace
    • Promotion suite and expanded supply for Vrbo
    • Announced intent to acquire Tiqets to enhance B2B and B2C offerings
